What is btcfi
## Unlock trillions in idle Bitcoin liquidity
Bitcoin is the most secure store of value, but its financial utility is severely limited. BTCfi enables Bitcoin to do more—earn, lend, trade—without compromising its core principles.
The challenge
Why BTC needs BTCfi
Bitcoin was designed for security and decentralization, but this comes at the cost of flexibility and financial utility.
![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6425f546844727ce5fb9e5ab/67e6d95b5b58faf9ae630b6a_Icon.svg)
No smart contracts
BTC lacks the programmability needed for advanced DeFi use cases.
![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6425f546844727ce5fb9e5ab/67e6d95b5b58faf9ae630b6a_Icon.svg)
Slow transactions
10-minute block times make Bitcoin impractical for high-speed financial applications.
![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6425f546844727ce5fb9e5ab/67e6d95b5b58faf9ae630b6a_Icon.svg)
Limited interoperability
BTC cannot seamlessly interact with other blockchain ecosystems, restricting its usability.
The solution
BTCfi changes this
BTCfi bridges the gap by making Bitcoin usable in DeFi without sacrificing security.
![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6425f546844727ce5fb9e5ab/67e6da40b1763779ca3b57e5_24x24.svg)
Earn yield
Put BTC to work via staking, lending, and liquidity pools.
![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6425f546844727ce5fb9e5ab/67e6da40df473ac95a16480f_24x24.svg)
Trade & borrow
Use BTC as collateral without selling.
![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6425f546844727ce5fb9e5ab/67e6da40460750ca3b1272b0_24x24.svg)
Trust-minimized interoperability
Move BTC into DeFi with fewer intermediaries.
Why btcfi on sui
